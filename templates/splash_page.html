Welcome to Meetup Organizer

Ever wanted to organize an event but couldn't find a fast, efficient way to get the information out and everyone on the same page?

Well we're here to help! Meetup Organizer creates a quick and efficient way for making events, inviting your friends, and creating a discussion channel for you to make posts and talk to each other about all the fun and excitement you'll have!

Best of all, it's free! Sign up today and see what we're all about

Sign up Log in
Create events, inviting friends to join and participate in a message board discussion. The event owner can then make announcements that display at the top for extra visibility.
Notifications are sent to each person, showing a list of all events that person has been invited to, while also showing a pending tab for those that wish to accept them.
The main page shows off your profile, showing your name, location, hobbies, and description of yourself while showing a condensed list of the events coming up.
